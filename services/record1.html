    FAQs

    What is Healthify

    Healthify is a web application that allows users to track their health data, access various services through a click away . Having some extra information towards your medical system can save a life and we provide that extra information. It provides an easy for you.

    What does record contain?

    Healthify record contains all your previous medical history. It contains all the original preception given by doctor and also all the test report of yours . Simply containg all the required document when a treatement starts or end.

    How to upload the record?

    You don't need to upload any document. You have to just make an Healthify accout which will allocate you an unique number or say ID . By at the time of consultation you have to just provide it to hospital rest will be take care by hospital.

    Can everyone see my record?

    No, only you can see your own records. To protect your privacy and confidentiality only person with unique ID and password can see but can have a glance at very basic info of you .

    What if doctor uploads a misleading report?

    If the uploaded document is found to be misleading or inaccurate, we will remove. And you a right to file a case against such case. Before final submission your approval will be required.

    Can I download it ?

    Yes, You can print or save the document for your record. But it will be not valid at medical store instead of that you can simply show the pdf to the required person.
